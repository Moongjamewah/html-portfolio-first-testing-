The Best Movies According to Jame

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather (1972)

Genre: Crime, Drama

A powerful crime drama directed by Francis Ford Coppola, "The Godfather" chronicles the life and legacy of the Corleone family, led by patriarch Vito Corleone. It explores themes of power, loyalty, and family dynamics within the context of organized crime.

Titanic (1997)

Genre: Romance, Drama

Directed by James Cameron, "Titanic" is a sweeping romantic drama set against the backdrop of the infamous 1912 maritime disaster. The film tells the poignant love story of Jack and Rose, passengers from different social classes, aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic.
